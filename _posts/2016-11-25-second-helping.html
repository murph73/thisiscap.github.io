Second Helping makes it easy to get quality, organic baby food, delivered to your doorstep.

What sets them apart from others, is their pledge to match every subscription with a donation of the same high quality baby food to national food banks.

A new venture, testing the waters for interest, this project wasn't the time for a full branding exercise. It was important though to establish, if only top line, a visual language.

Website

The importance of measurement and exposure lent itself to Launchrock, a platform dedicated to early customer acquisition.

Website landing page

The bespoke design was applied to the Launchrock campaign to make use of its tools, while maintaining a high level of control over the site.

Campaign visuals

Working with strategist John Graham we put together a variety of creative for Second Helping awareness campaigns.
